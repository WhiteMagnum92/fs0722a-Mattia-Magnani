Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,
crenberries, and brie

wow you taste buds with this focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and cremy brie. It will become your favorite holiday starter!


Image: Sally Vegas

FEATURED IN:

fresh cranberry recipes that go beyohnd sauce

Ingredients

  • 4 cups (500g) all purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons istant yeas
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh of frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix toghether all
the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to ctually knead the
dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate
the last of the flour...
